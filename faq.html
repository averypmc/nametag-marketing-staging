Can I trial nametag before paying?

Sure! Just add us to your Slack workspace (no credit card required) and set us up in 1 minute. Simply pick a channel, and nametag will DM members of that channel to see if they want to opt into weekly summaries of new people they DMed.

You can configure if you want to match people who share a DM for the first time ever or for the first time since nametag was installed. The latter is useful if you want people who know each other (but probably not well üëÄ) to make time for a 1:1 chat.

How does pricing work?

We charge a base fee of $30/month charged monthly to for access to nametag. This includes up to 15 active users, and each additional active user is $1/month.
‚Äç
An active user is someone who is opted in to nametag's weekly summaries. If someone opts-in on March 15th and your billing cycle starts on March 1st, we won't charge you until April 1st. Similarly, if someone opts out on March 15th, you'll have paid $1 for that user for March but they'll be removed from your April charge.

Our average opt-in rate is 50%, so for a workspace of 100 people with 50 opted in, you can expect to pay $65 monthly.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! nametag cannot view or read private DMs or private channels in your workspace. In public channels, we can only view direct responses to the nametag bot.

When you uninstall the app, all data apart from your email and the name of your Slack team is removed (any data can be removed upon request). Read more about our privacy policy at nametag.work/privacy.
